Plan Details

Movie Nights — Full Breakdown

Over the last few years, we've had Movie Night once a month, and they've always been the backbone of our community. We vote on a movie earlier in the day, order pizza, set up some blankets, pillows, and a projector, and sit back and enjoy the movie. It's one of my favorite Fusion traditions, and I would love to continue it this year.

Snack Cart - Full Breakdown

I've noticed that sometimes students get tired or have difficulty focusing during school. Often, this is a result of students not eating anything. If we implement a complementary Snack Cart, students would have easy access to fuel to study and function more efficiently, which would greatly improve the individual experience of students.

Holiday Decorations - Full Breakdown

We've always gone all out with holiday decorations, and that's not gonna stop this year. For Halloween, Christmas, Valentine's Day and more, we will create, design, and implement decorations, showcasing our individual creative talents as students, as well as celebrating these holidays as a community.

Additional Ideas

Super Semester Start Sunrise

At the start of the year, we will have a small event where we wake up early, go to the top of the parking structure, play some games and enjoy some food, and watch the sunrise.
